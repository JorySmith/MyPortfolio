    About me

    Why consider me when you have so many options?

    I can deliver your vision

    I'm Jory Smith, Full Stack Web Developer

    I help craft your vision into professional, stylish, fast, secure, and scalable websites and applications that deliver UX results. My tech stack includes, C#, ASP.NET MVC, React, JavaScript, SQL, Bootstrap, Sass, and Figma.

    I'm also a veteran and former lead intelligence analyst with five combat deployments supporting Special Operations Forces. I specialized in solving complex problems, rapid prototyping, quickly adapting to changing requirements, and elevating teams to deliver innovative solutions that typically exceeded client expectations. My intel background includes:

    • Lead analyst for up to 30-person teams that provided critical research, data analysis, and products.
    • Expert in adapting to rapidly evolving requirements, technology workflows, and stringent deadlines.
    • Adept at project management software such as Atlassian Jira for tracking hundreds of requirements.
    • Bestowed several awards for prototype innovation, customer support, and operational excellence.
